# Problem Statement — Kasparro Agentic Commerce Hackathon | April 2026

**Contact:** grandmaster@kasparro.com

---

## Track 1: AI Shopping Agent

**Goal:** Build an AI shopping agent that helps users discover the right products across Shopify merchants and move from intent to purchase.

---

## What Kasparro Wants

Think of this as **replacing the entire browse-search-filter-compare loop with a single conversation.**

The agent should:
- Understand what the user actually needs
- Narrow options intelligently
- Compare products on dimensions that matter
- Explain its recommendations clearly
- Support a clean path toward cart or checkout

---

## Example User Intents

- "Help me find running shoes for flat feet under ₹5,000"
- "I need a gift for my mom who likes skincare but is picky about ingredients"
- "Build me a complete skincare routine for oily skin"

---

## What a Strong Submission Looks Like

- **Understands user intent deeply** — asks smart follow-up questions instead of showing everything
- **Narrows options with clear reasoning** — not just keyword matching
- **Handles tradeoffs explicitly** — price vs. quality, availability vs. preference
- **Explains why it recommends what it recommends**
- **Supports a clean path toward purchase** — not just a product list

---

## Visual Search — How to Use It

The Curio AI supports image-based search (upload a photo → get matching products).

**Where to get images:**
- Screenshot any outfit from **Instagram**, **Pinterest**, **Myntra**, **Zara**, or any fashion website
- Take a photo of clothing you already own
- Screenshot a product from within the app itself

**What to ask after uploading:**
- "Find me something similar to this"
- "What style is this outfit?"
- "I love this vibe — what else would go with it?"
- "Find this under ₹3,000"
- "What occasions would this work for?"
